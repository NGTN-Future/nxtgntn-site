Who We Are

NXTGNTN trains college students to become the next generation of Tennessee's civic and political leaders. Through campus chapters, structured curriculum, and hands-on organizing experience, we're building a statewide pipeline of leaders who understand how to run campaigns, hold office, and serve their communities.

We're explicitly nonpartisan in everything we do — our focus is on developing the skills, character, and civic knowledge that leadership requires, not any political ideology. Founded in 2025, our founding chapter launches at the University of Tennessee Martin in August 2026, with statewide expansion to follow.

Our Mission

Civic education and political leadership training for college students across Tennessee — building an army of trained, nonpartisan leaders ready to serve their communities and run for office.
